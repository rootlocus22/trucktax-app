## Key Talking Points & Preparation Guide

### 1. **About Your Company (ResumeGyani)**
- **Current user base and growth metrics**
- **Key features and differentiators** of your resume builder
- **Technology stack and platform capabilities**
- **Success stories/testimonials** from users who got jobs
- **Mobile app availability** (if applicable)
- **Pricing model** (free, freemium, paid tiers)

### 2. **Questions to Ask Shine.com**

#### **Partnership Structure:**
- What type of partnership are you envisioning? (White-label, API integration, co-branded solution, referral program?)
- Will this be exclusive or non-exclusive?
- What is the expected timeline for launch?

#### **Technical Integration:**
- What are your technical requirements and constraints?
- Do you have an API or integration framework we should be aware of?
- What level of customization/branding do you need?
- Will users be redirected to ResumeGyani or embedded within Shine.com?

#### **Business Model:**
- How will revenue be shared (if applicable)?
- Will Shine.com users get free access, discounted pricing, or premium features?
- What are your expectations regarding user acquisition costs?
- Are there any minimum user volume commitments?

#### **User Experience:**
- How do you envision this fitting into the Shine.com user journey?
- At what point in the job search process should resume building be introduced?
- What metrics matter most to Shine.com? (User engagement, conversion, job placement rates?)

#### **Support & Operations:**
- What level of support and maintenance is expected?
- Who will handle customer support for resume-related queries?
- Are there any compliance or data privacy requirements specific to Shine.com?

### 3. **What to Prepare Before the Call**

#### **Demo Preparation:**
- **Live demo** of your resume builder platform
- **Sample resumes** created using your platform
- **Feature walkthrough** highlighting unique capabilities
- **Mobile experience** demonstration (if applicable)
- **Admin dashboard** overview (if relevant for partnership)

#### **Documentation:**
- **Product overview deck** (10-15 slides)
- **API documentation** (if technical integration is discussed)
- **Pricing sheet** with partnership pricing options
- **Case studies** or success metrics
- **Technical architecture** overview (high-level)

#### **Business Information:**
- **Partnership agreement template** or key terms you'd like to discuss
- **Revenue sharing model** proposals
- **Integration timeline** estimates
- **Support SLA** details

### 4. **Potential Scenarios & Responses**

#### **Scenario 1: They want white-label solution**
- **Your response:** "We can provide a white-label solution. Let me understand your branding requirements and technical infrastructure to provide accurate timelines and pricing."

#### **Scenario 2: They want API integration**
- **Your response:** "We have robust APIs available. I'll have our technical team prepare integration documentation and discuss authentication, rate limits, and data flow."

#### **Scenario 3: They want exclusive partnership**
- **Your response:** "An exclusive partnership would require careful consideration. Let's discuss the terms, duration, and mutual commitments that would make this viable for both parties."

#### **Scenario 4: They want free access for their users**
- **Your response:** "We can explore various models - freemium with premium upsells, volume-based pricing, or revenue-sharing. What's your user engagement expectation?"

#### **Scenario 5: They ask about your user base**
- **Your response:** [Be prepared with actual numbers] "We currently serve [X] users with [Y]% growth rate. Our users have created [Z] resumes and have a [A]% job placement rate."

#### **Scenario 6: They want to move fast**
- **Your response:** "We can accelerate the timeline. Let me outline a phased approach - we can start with a pilot program and scale based on results."

### 5. **Red Flags to Watch For**

- **Unrealistic expectations** (e.g., expecting free service for millions of users)
- **Exclusivity without commitment** (exclusive partnership without minimum guarantees)
- **Complex integration** with tight deadlines and no budget
- **Data ownership concerns** (who owns user data?)
- **Unclear decision-making process** (who has final say?)

### 6. **Your Negotiation Points**

- **Value proposition:** Emphasize the quality and effectiveness of your resume builder
- **User success:** Highlight job placement rates and user testimonials
- **Technology:** Showcase your platform's reliability and scalability
- **Support:** Demonstrate your commitment to user success
- **Flexibility:** Show willingness to customize for their needs

### 7. **Follow-Up Actions After Call**

- Send thank-you email within 24 hours
- Share promised documentation (demo access, pricing, technical specs)
- Propose next steps and timeline
- Schedule follow-up meeting if needed
- Internal debrief with your team

### 8. **Key Metrics to Share (If Asked)**

- **User acquisition cost** (if comfortable sharing)
- **User retention rate**
- **Average time to create a resume**
- **Job placement success rate**
- **Platform uptime/reliability**
- **Customer satisfaction scores**

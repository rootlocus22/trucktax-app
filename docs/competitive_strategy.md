## 2. Content Strategy & Sitemap Insights
Analysis of their sitemap reveals targeted niche clusters we are missing:

### A. High-Value Service Pages
They have dedicated landing pages for specific filing types that we should replicate:
*   **"Free VIN Correction"**: A major pain point. We should have a dedicated page for this.
*   **"Form 8849 / Refunds"**: They have a specific flow for claiming tax refunds (sold/destroyed vehicles).
*   **"Suspended Vehicle Filing"**: Targeting low-mileage trucks (Category W).
*   **"Amendments"**: Specifically for "Mileage Increase" and "Gross Weight Increase".

### B. The "State Authority" Gap
They do **not** have dedicated state landing pages (e.g., `/texas-2290`).
*   **Strategy**: Create 50 state-specific guide pages (e.g., "Texas HVUT Filing Guide", "California Form 2290 Rules") to dominate local search queries where they are weak.

### C. Niche Vehicle Types
They target specific industries:
*   **Logging Vehicles**: Lower tax rates.
*   **Agricultural Vehicles**: Exemptions and special mileage rules (%7,500 miles).
